Chapter 5: Global Manipulations and the Brink of War

On the global stage, the "bad" party ignites conflicts, pushing the world toward the brink of war. They support oppressive regimes, fund dubious alliances, and steer the globe toward the precipice of World War III. In response, the "good" side's candidate emerges as the sole leader capable of steering the world away from destruction, speaking passionately of peace and unity. Desperate to end the chaos and terror that the "bad" side has engineered, people flock to him, unknowingly falling into the ultimate deception.
